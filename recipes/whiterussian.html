White Russian

A Three-Ingredient Cocktail!

The White Rusian is a very easy cocktail to make. Combining vodka, Kahlua and cream and serving it on the rocks makes a great alternative to an adult milkshake.

  • 2 ounces Vodka
  • 1 ounce Kahlua
  • 1 splash heavy cream
  • Some ice
  1. Put ice in a glass.
  2. Add the vodka and Kahlua.
  3. Top with heavy cream.
  4. Stir, and enjoy!
