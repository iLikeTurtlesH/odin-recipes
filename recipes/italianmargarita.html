Italian Margarita

This cocktail only takes four ingredients!

An Italian margarita is a simple twist on the classic. It's made with tequila and fresh lime juice. You can use many ingredients along with those, such as triple sec, Cointreau, or amaretto.

  • Orange Juice
  • Lime Juice
  • Tequila
  • Amaretto
  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Add all the ingredients above.
  3. Shake well and strain into a glass.
